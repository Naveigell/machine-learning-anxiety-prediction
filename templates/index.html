Anxiety Prediction System

Machine Learning-based Anxiety Detection

Important Disclaimer: This tool is built for learning and portfolio purposes only and should not be used for clinical or medical decisions. If you or someone you know is experiencing anxiety, please consult a qualified mental health professional.

Model Limitations: This model was trained on a highly imbalanced dataset where only 7.8% of samples (122 out of 1,558) are labeled as anxiety. SMOTE oversampling was applied to address class imbalance during training, which improved test set performance (Recall=0.92, F1=0.96) but introduced overfitting on training data. As a result, the model may struggle to generalize to new inputs and could predict No Anxiety even for profiles that clinically suggest otherwise. These limitations are acknowledged as part of the learning process.
